ShowWork Assignment review
An AI vulnerability review for assignments. Based off of AUPHA Institute for Learning course by Eric Richardson, Jean Gordon, Dawn Oetjen, and Reid Oetjen, ShowWork has been expanded to provide specific guidance based on the type of assigment being reviewed. Paste in an assignment prompt and rubric, choose the assignment type, and get a structured review of where student reasoning might be invisible, with specific suggestions for revision.
